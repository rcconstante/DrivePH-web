DrivePH Guide

DrivePH Guide is an independent Philippine driving education app for student permit preparation, driver license review, LTO-style reviewer quizzes, road signs, traffic rules, driving scenarios, and vehicle care.

The app is built for Filipino drivers who want a focused, mobile-first guide before studying official LTO requirements, practicing road safety, or reviewing common driving topics.

What DrivePH Guide Covers

  • Student permit basics and Philippine driver license topics.
  • LTO reviewer-style quizzes, final exam practice, and topic checkpoints.
  • Road signs, traffic signals, pavement markings, right-of-way, parking, and crash duties.
  • Real-road driving scenarios for hazards, intersections, parking, rain, traffic flow, and defensive driving.
  • Vehicle care lessons covering basic checks, maintenance, safety, and owner responsibilities.

Independent Educational Notice

DrivePH Guide is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Land Transportation Office or any government agency. Always verify current rules, fees, forms, procedures, and requirements with official government sources before acting on them.
